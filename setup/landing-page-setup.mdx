Our application comes with a highly customizable landing page. As an administrator, you can control the content, branding, and layout of the public-facing site.

### Accessing Landing Page Settings

To configure your landing page, you must be logged in as an **administrator**.

1.  Navigate to **Settings** in the main sidebar.
2.  Go to **Admin** > **Landing**.

### General Settings

- **Enable Landing Page**: Toggle this on to show the landing page to visitors. When disabled, all visitors will be automatically redirected to the login page.

### Header / Hero Section

This section is the first thing visitors see.

- **Title**: The main headline (e.g., `Grow Organic Traffic on Auto-Pilot`).
- **Subtitle**: A secondary headline (e.g., `Get done-for-you Blog Posts that actually rank.`).
- **Description**: A detailed explanation of your service.
- **Call-to-Action Link**: The destination URL for the main button (e.g., `/login`).
- **YouTube Video URL**: Optional link to a demo video (e.g., `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nFGnWc4s7qU`).

### Layout & Design

- **Layout**: Choose your preferred layout style (e.g., `Layout 1 - Image Right`).
- **Gradient Background**: Customize the hero section's background gradient using the following controls:
    - **From color**: The starting color of the gradient.
    - **From stop**: The percentage where the first color ends (e.g., `28%`).
    - **Via color**: An optional middle color.
    - **Via stop**: The percentage for the middle color (e.g., `70%`).
    - **To color**: The ending color of the gradient.

### Footer

- **Footer Text**: The copyright or informational text at the bottom of the page (e.g., `Â© 2026 SuperSaas. All rights reserved.`).

### Saving Changes

Click **Save Settings** at the bottom of the page to apply your changes instantly to the public landing page.

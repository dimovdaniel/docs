If you are using the **SaaS version** of the application, you can manage pricing plans and configure your subscription payment provider from the admin dashboard.

### Accessing Subscription Settings

To manage your pricing and payments, you must be logged in as an **administrator**.

1.  Navigate to **Settings** in the main sidebar.
2.  Go to **Admin** > **Subscriptions**.

### Subscription Settings

Choose your preferred subscription payment provider. Each plan's **External ID** in our application must match a product or price ID in your chosen provider's dashboard.

### Managing Plans Manually

As an administrator, you have the flexibility to override subscription settings for specific companies.

1.  Navigate to the **Companies** list.
2.  Select a specific company.
3.  You can manually set or change the **Pricing Plan** for that company regardless of their current billing status.

### Creating Pricing Plans

In the **Admin > Plans** section, you can define your tiers:
- **Name**: The display name of the plan (e.g., Pro, Enterprise).
- **External ID**: The ID from your payment provider (Lemon Squeezy, Polar, or Paddle).
- **Features**: Define which features are enabled for this specific tier.
